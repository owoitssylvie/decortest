Submitting Your Decors

To submit your decors, you will need to contact me on Discord. You can join the Decor Server and then ping me in the #decoration-discussion channel.

Please follow these guidelines as well as Decor's Guidelines:

1. Must be YOUR OWN work. Stolen/unoriginal decorations will be denied.

2. Must have a particular theme/category, rather than several unrelated decors.

3. Must submit a minimum of 6 decors per submission. I'd rather not create a new category and banner just for 1 or 2 decorations.

4. Use of AI is okay, but decorations may be removed if an artist claims the AI image as trained on their art without permission.

5. Must use the template to ensure correct sizing. Please try to avoid the red and blue areas.

 

Decoration already exists

Decor stores the images in a server; so if the exact same image gets uploaded twice, it'll error because the metadata matches an existing one. You'll need to change this metadata to upload the decor.

One way of doing this, is to use this site, or you can go to ezgif and resize it by like 1%. Both options work.
